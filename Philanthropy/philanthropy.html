PHA 2019-2020 Philanthropy

Scott County Humane Society

The Scott County Humane Society is a non-profit community located in Scott County, Kentucky. Our mission is to ensure humane care and conditions for companion animals through spay/neuter, humane awareness education, and responsible pet adoption and ownership.

PHA's Involvement

Our fraternity will be volunteering our time to help with daily tasks at the shelter, as well as raising money to donate to the operation expenses of the shelter.
